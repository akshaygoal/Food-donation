About Us

WHO WE ARE

Happy tummy is a website that connects donors and needy people. Our main motto is to discard the idea of food wastage.

WHAT WE DO

We provide multiple stalls across banglore where the donors can donate surplus food which will be helpful to many around them. Donors can donate any eatables and they will be provided with shopping coupons according to the donations recieved.
